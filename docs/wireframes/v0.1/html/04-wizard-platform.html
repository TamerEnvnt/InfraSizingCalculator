TIP
Which should I choose?
Choose based on your development team's skills and your organization's existing technology stack. Low-code platforms offer faster development but may have higher licensing costs.

Component Specifications

Element Specifications Behavior

Interaction Notes

  • Only one platform can be selected at a time
  • Selection persists when navigating back from later steps
  • Changing platform resets subsequent wizard steps
  • Keyboard: Tab between cards, Enter/Space to select
  • Screen reader: Announce "Platform type, radio group, 2 options"
